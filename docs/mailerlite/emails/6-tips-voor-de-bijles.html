Kies een vast dagdeel
Dezelfde dag, hetzelfde tijdstip elke week. Routine maakt van bijles een vast onderdeel van de week, in plaats van iets wat steeds opnieuw ingepland moet worden.

Pick a fixed time slot
Same day, same time every week. Routine turns tutoring into a fixed part of the week.
Let {$student_name} review for 10 minutes daily
One hour a week followed by nothing doesn’t work. A short daily review is far more effective than a marathon right before a test.
Provide a quiet environment
Phone away, TV off. Distraction directly affects what {$student_name} takes in during the lesson.
Share test dates and what {$student_name} needs
Let {$teacher_name} know in time when tests are coming. Don’t wait until a week beforehand.
A bigger gap or an important test coming up?
One hour a week is often not enough then. Book extra hours so {$teacher_name} can really make a difference.
Plan ahead and always book a next lesson
Plan lessons 4 weeks ahead for a steady rhythm. Then book the next lesson right at the end of each one.
Set a goal per period
What does {$student_name} want to achieve in the coming 6 weeks? A concrete goal gives direction to every lesson.
